Our Apps

Aura Browser

Aura is a web browser designed for iPad with additional support for iPhone, Mac, and Vision Pro. The app is designed as a customizable and open source alternative to Arc browser. The browser is currently available in beta. You can view the code and contribute on GitHub.

DH Chat

DH Chat is a messaging platform for groups. Our focus with this app was to create a more powerful alternative to GroupMe that maintains simplicity. It is essentially in between GroupMe and Discord. It is not available yet. When it is released, it will support iOS, Android, and Web.

SF Folder Icons

This is one of our simpler apps. It allows you to customize the folder icons on your Mac. You can easily change the colors and add icons from a library of over 6000 symbols. You can also upload your own images to create custom folder designs. It is available on the App Store for $0.99 or on GitHub for free.

CelsiUSA

CelsiUSA is our app to help you switch from Fahrenheit to Celsius. While most of the world is already using Celsius, the US still uses Fahrenheit. This app is designed to help you switch to Celsius. The app displays temperature in metric, but simple gestures convert the measurements to imperial units.

Download Learn More

DH Parkour

DoorHinge Parkour is our first app we developed. It is designed to teach you parkour in simple progressions. Parkour is difficult to learn, but this app helps make it a bit easier with videos and descriptions to walk you through skills. DoorHinge Parkour is the winner of the 2022 Congressional App Challenge for Oklahoma District 1.
